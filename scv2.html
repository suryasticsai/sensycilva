What is SensyCilva?

SensyCilva is an AI-powered anime pirate radio with a live host named Sensy. She broadcasts anime news, recommendations, and trivia — and you can talk back to her in real time in English, Hindi, or Telugu.

Getting Started

1
Choose Your Language
Pick English 🇬🇧, Hindi 🇮🇳, or Telugu 🇮🇳 from the top bar. Sensy will speak and understand in that language.
2
Hit GO ON AIR
Sensy auto-generates a fresh anime broadcast script using AI, then reads it aloud with animated lip-sync and oscilloscope.
3
Talk Back to Sensy
Type or use 🎤 mic. Ask about anime, request a song, ask trivia, or just chat. Sensy pauses the broadcast, responds, then resumes automatically.
4
Play Music (optional)
Add a free YouTube Data API v3 key in the Music Player section. Then say "play Naruto OST" or any anime song. Get a key at console.cloud.google.com
