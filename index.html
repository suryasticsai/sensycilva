What is SensyCilva Radio?

SensyCilva Radio is an AI-powered multi-language broadcast experience with a live AI host named Sensy. She talks anime news, recommendations, and trivia — and you can talk back to her in English, Hindi, or Telugu, any time.

Getting Started

1
Choose Your Language
Pick English, Hindi, or Telugu. Each channel has its own unique theme, colors, and vibe. Sensy speaks your language!
2
Hit GO ON AIR
Sensy generates a fresh broadcast script using AI, then reads it aloud with animated lip-sync and a live oscilloscope waveform.
3
Talk Back to Sensy
Type or use 🎤 mic. Sensy pauses the broadcast, has a real conversation with you, then resumes automatically. She'll ask where you're calling from!
4
Play Music (Optional)
Add a free YouTube Data API v3 key. Then say "play Naruto OST" — Sensy queues it up! Get a key at console.cloud.google.com
